Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions our clients ask us.

What services does RRR 360 Real Estate provide?

We offer property sales, rentals, property management, investment consulting, and real estate marketing services across Jordan.

How can I list my property on the website?

Simply contact our team, provide property details, images, and documents. We will create and publish your listing on the platform.

Do you handle both sales and rentals?

Yes, we manage properties for both selling and renting, including residential, commercial, lands, and office spaces.

Is there a fee for buyers or tenants?

Our commission structure varies depending on the property type. Buyers and tenants usually pay a small percentage or fixed fee after the deal is confirmed.

Can I request a property that is not listed?

Absolutely! Share your requirements with us, and our team will find matching properties through our extended real estate network.

How do I schedule a property visit?

You can book a visit directly through the property page or by contacting our agents. We will arrange a suitable time for you to view the property.
